# Nivix Example: Hierarchical Layout
# Prompt: Explain the relationship between the numerator and denominator in a fraction.

### 1. INPUT (prompt)
"Explain the relationship between the numerator (top) and denominator (bottom) in a fraction."

### 2. SYMBOLIC ANALYSIS
- Intent: Describe_Relation(Numerator, Denominator)
- Hierarchy: Vertical_Layout (Numerator above Denominator)
- Group: Fraction_Group(Numerator, Denominator, Fraction_Bar)
- Anchor: Label("top") -> Numerator, Label("bottom") -> Denominator

### 3. CIR EXPECTATION (v3.9)
- Spawn(Numerator, text='Numerator')
- Spawn(Denominator, text='Denominator')
- Align(Numerator, Denominator, axis='vertical')
- Group(Numerator, Denominator, name='fraction')
- Highlight(Numerator, duration=2.5)
- Highlight(Denominator, duration=2.5)

### 4. PEDAGOGICAL SCORE
- Hierarchy Score: 1.0 (Audit verified)
- Group Membership: Verified (Fraction_Group exists)
